1. how to trigger the event

Click anywhere where there is not an entity.

2. what should happen when the event is triggered

A lake will be randomly generated from four shape options. After a random period of time, the center of the lake will begin
to animate. A serpent will eventually spawn and seek out the nearest Dude (Full or NotFull). After it has "eaten" the Dude,
it will return to it's spawn point and submerge under the waves.

3. what the affected entities should do

The nearest fairy will turn blue and eat water.

4. what the new entity is and how it should behave

The new entity is the serpent and it's behavior is described above.
